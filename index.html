The First Glimpse

It wasn't just about looking up; it was about understanding the scale of it all. The silence of the night sky in Mallorca offered a perspective that the noise of the day could never provide. I realized that every photon hitting my sensor had traveled thousands of years just to end its journey here.

The Technical Pursuit

Astrophotography is where art meets rigorous science. It taught me patience—collecting light for hours to reveal what the human eye cannot see. Just like in Data Science, it is about extracting signal from noise, finding patterns in the chaos of the cosmos using tools like PixInsight and Python.

The Philosophy of Light

We are merely temporary observers of an eternal universe. Through my lens, I attempt to capture these fleeting moments of eternity. Whether it is the craters of the Moon or the dust lanes of Andromeda, each image is a humbled acknowledgement of our place in the void.
